Capital Governorate's Unexplored Destinations

HISTORY

The Capital Governorate of Bahrain, established in 2002, includes Manama, Bahrain's capital. Historically, Manama has been the political, economic, and cultural hub, with roots in ancient trading routes and pearling. Modern developments began in the early 20th century, transforming it into a financial and administrative center.























CULTURE

The culture of Bahrain's Capital Governorate blends tradition and modernity. Influenced by Arab, Persian, and Indian cultures, it showcases Islamic customs, vibrant souqs, and pearl diving heritage. Manama hosts art galleries, festivals, and diverse cuisine, reflecting Bahrain's cosmopolitan nature while preserving its Bedouin roots and rich Gulf traditions.
